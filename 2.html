"I wish this was our daily bus,
We would sit the two of us.
You would say its just a dream,
I guess you’re right and so it seams...

I dreamed a beautiful thing.

I wish this was our daily route.
I would stand and play the flute.
The bus would dance, sing and salute
If this is a dream then its mighty cute.

I dreamed a beautiful thing.
I found you in a dream,
So the best thing I can do
Is go back to sleep...
And seek....
You out.
